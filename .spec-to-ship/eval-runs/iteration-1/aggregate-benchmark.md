## Aggregate

- Baseline: 45/96 (46.9%)
- With skill: 95/96 (99.0%)
- Delta: 52.1 percentage points

## Caveats

- Baseline and with-skill responses were generated by fresh skill-maker subagents per skill, with baselines produced before target SKILL.md was read.
- Grading was manual/semantic by the eval subagents with evidence in grading.json.
- Timing files are placeholders because exact child timing was unavailable.

## High-value improvement signals

- `finish`, `tdd`, and `coding-agent` showed the largest deltas, validating STS-specific cleanup, red-green, and planning gates.
- `ui-ux-gate` had the smallest delta because baseline responses handled two near-miss/review cases well; future evals should be more discriminating around Impeccable evidence artifacts.
- `debug` was the only with-skill result below 100%, indicating the skill needs stronger no-fixture/no-root-cause-proof behavior.
- Some near-miss evals are intentionally non-discriminating to test over-trigger resistance; they should remain, but be complemented with harder baseline-failing cases.
